Biography

I’m a PhD student in the Computer Vision and Geometry Group at ETH Zurich under the supervision of Prof. Marc Pollefeys.

I obtained both my BSc in Computer Science and MSc in Robotics, Systems and Control from ETH Zurich and spent a semester at KTH in Stockholm. I also spent some time at Volkswagen Group Research, the Microsoft Mixed Reality & AI Lab, and Meta Reality Labs as an intern.
